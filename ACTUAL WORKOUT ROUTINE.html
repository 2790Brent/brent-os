Nutrition System
Fueling sub-3 marathon + dunking + 500 push-ups · Target body: 163–168 lb
Eat Like an Athlete
Dropping from 177 → 163–168 lb is your biggest performance lever. Every 5 lb lost = ~30 sec/mile faster, 2–3 inches higher jump, more push-up reps. Don't rush it — lose no more than 1–1.5 lb/week.

Meal Structure
Meal 1 — Breakfast
45–55g protein · 70–90g carbs
4 eggs + egg whites + oats + banana + Greek yogurt. Or protein smoothie + oats.
Pre-Workout — 60–90 min before
30–40g protein · 60–80g carbs · low fat
Rice cakes + honey + whey shake + banana. Toast + honey + Greek yogurt.
Post-Workout — within 30 min
40–50g protein · 80–110g carbs
Whey + cereal or white rice + fruit. Chicken + rice. Fast-digesting carbs priority.
Meal 3 — Lunch
50–60g protein · 80–100g carbs
Chicken + white rice + roasted veg + olive oil. Steak + potatoes + salad.
Dinner
45–55g protein · carbs based on day type
Salmon or lean beef + potatoes + salad + olive oil. Chicken pasta. Turkey tacos.
Supplement Stack
Creatine
5g/day, every day. Non-negotiable for all 3 goals.
Whey Protein
1–2 scoops/day to hit 185–190g protein target.
Caffeine
150–200mg pre hard day only. Skip easy days.
Electrolytes
Every Zone 2 + long run day. Sweat is real in Florida.
Vitamin D3 + K2
2,000–4,000 IU daily. Joint + hormonal health.
Fish Oil
2–3g EPA/DHA. Reduces inflammation from high volume.
